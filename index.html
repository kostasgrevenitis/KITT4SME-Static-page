Objective

The KITT4SME (platform-enabled KITs of arTificial intelligence FOR an easy uptake by SMEs) specifically targets European SMEs and mid-caps to provide them with scope-tailored and industry-ready hardware, software and organisational kits, delivered as modularly customisable digital platform, that seamlessly introduce artificial intelligence in their production systems.

Uptake of the resulting packages and of the provided devices is strongly supported by the clear characterisation and market readiness of the individual components as well as by the platform grounding on the already established RAMP Marketplace. Leverage on the network of Digital Innovation Hubs, three of which are represented in the consortium, ensure that KITT4SME are widely distributed to a wide audience of companies in Europe. Seamless adoption of the customised kits is made possible by a Powered by FIWARE infrastructure that flawlessly combine Factory systems (such as MES and ERP), IoT sensors and wearable devices, robots, collaborative robots and other Factory data sources with functional modules capable to trigger data-driven value creation.

The KITT4SME concept comes into being from the acknowledge that companies are unprepared and inadequately exposed to extremely complex and widely diverse digital solutions in the field of artificial intelligence that are often much too broad in scope and relentless in dictating their own objectives instead of focusing on the issues a company really faces. Yet, whereas big companies have capitals and resources to research and build platforms internally, SMEs struggle in finding solutions that are fitting to their needs and, even when exposed to them, have to bear significant financial risks to embrace them while lacking the competences for a first-right selection. Lag in the digitisation race for them is not a system by-product it is a designed feature.

Who and why should invest in KITT4SME

Who should invest in KITT4SME?

Manufacturing SMEs that would benefit from and be interested in using a platform like KITT4SME typically share certain characteristics and face specific challenges that the platform is designed to address. Here are the types of manufacturing companies that would likely be attracted to KITT4SME:

  • Traditional Industries: Manufacturers in sectors such as textiles, automotive parts, machinery, and consumer goods, which might not have a high level of digitization, can significantly benefit from the tailored AI solutions that KITT4SME offers.
  • Custom and Specialty Manufacturers: Businesses that produce custom or small-batch products can use KITT4SME to optimize their production processes, manage resources better, and improve product quality without the need for large-scale investments.
  • Manufacturers with Complex Supply Chains: Companies dealing with intricate supply chains can use the platform to enhance their supply chain management through improved data integration, predictive analytics, and real-time monitoring.
  • High-Mix, Low-Volume Manufacturers: Manufacturers that produce a wide variety of products in small quantities can benefit from the modular and customizable nature of KITT4SME, allowing them to efficiently manage production variability.
  • Manufacturers Seeking Improved Data Utilization: Companies looking to leverage their existing data more effectively can use KITT4SME to integrate various data sources, such as IoT sensors and ERP systems, to drive better decision-making and operational efficiency.

Why should invest in KIT4SME platform?

  • Tailored Solutions: KITT4SME specifically targets small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and mid-sized companies, offering solutions that are tailored to the specific needs and constraints of these businesses. This customization ensures that the technology is not only relevant but also practical and accessible for smaller companies that may lack the resources to develop such solutions in-house.
  • Comprehensive AI Integration:The platform provides industry-ready hardware and software kits that seamlessly integrate artificial intelligence into existing production systems.
  • Modular and customizable: KITT4SME offers a modular approach, allowing companies to customize their digital transformation journey. This flexibility is crucial for SMEs that need to adopt technology incrementally and according to their unique business needs and budget constraints.
  • Established Market Readiness: The platform relies on the RAMP Marketplace, which is already established, ensuring that the components and solutions provided are market-ready. This reduces the risk associated with adopting new technologies and ensures reliability and support.
  • Powered by FIWARE Infrastructure and open-source software: The platform's use of the FIWARE infrastructure, and other open-source software solutions, ensures seamless integration of various factory data sources. This enables real-time data analysis and functional module triggering, which can drive significant value creation through enhanced operational efficiencies and smarter decision-making.
  • Deploy everywhere: It can be deployed on the cloud and on premises
